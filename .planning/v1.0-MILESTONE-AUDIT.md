## Cross-Phase Integration

### Broken Connection: Sermon Adapter → Outliner

**Severity: HIGH — breaks the sermon series E2E flow**

The orchestrator's Stage 0.5 invokes the sermon adapter which writes to `sources-adapted/`. The orchestrator then passes a plain-text instruction to the outliner to read from `sources-adapted/`. However, the outliner's SKILL.md hardcodes `sources/` in its mode detection (Section 2) and source reading (Section 4.1). It has no logic to check for or prefer `sources-adapted/`.

**Fix:** Update outliner SKILL.md Section 2 and Section 4.1 to check for `sources-adapted/` first when that directory exists with files.
