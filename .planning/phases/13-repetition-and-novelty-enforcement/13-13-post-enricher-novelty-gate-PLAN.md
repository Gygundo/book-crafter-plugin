<objective>
Add a post-enricher novelty gate (Stage 4.6) to the orchestrator. The SC-6 proof run revealed that the editor's §4.4.5 Novelty and Dedup Audit runs during Stage 4 — BEFORE the enricher generates the foreword at Stage 4.5. Stage 4.6 closes that gap by running craft-check.js --novelty against the full corpus (including the newly-generated foreword) after Stage 4.5 completes.
